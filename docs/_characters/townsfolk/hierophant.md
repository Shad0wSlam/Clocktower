---
layout: character
id: hierophant
summary: |
    The Hierophant knows who people should be thinking about.

    - The Hierophant chooses a player, and then learns 2 players.
    - The Hierophant does not know which of the 2 players should be considered and which should not.
    - The chosen and learned players can be alive or dead.
    - The chosen and learned players can be good or evil.
howtorun: |
  Each night, wake the Hierophant. They point to a player. Point to 2 players. Put the Hierophant to sleep.
examples:
  - On the first night, the Hierophant chooses the Fortune Teller. They learn the player who is the Fortune Teller's red herring, and the Recluse. On the second night, they choose the Goblin, and learn the Damsel and the [[Torpor]].
  - On the first night, the Hierophant chooses the Pixie, who saw the [[Cloudchaser]]. The Hierophant learns the Cloudchaser, who the Pixie can confirm as good, and the Balloonist, who is entirely unrelated.
